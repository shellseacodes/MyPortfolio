About Me

Hello! I'm Chelsey, a passionate and driven software engineer and illustrator based in Texas. At 25 years old, I am currently pursuing an Associates degree in Computer Science at Texas Southmost College. With a strong foundation in programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and C++, I have the technical skills to bring innovative ideas to life. Additionally, I possess a creative flair and proficiency in programs like Adobe, Clip Studio, and Procreate, allowing me to create captivating illustrations. I strive to combine my artistic abilities with my programming expertise to develop visually stunning and functional applications. My dedication to continuous learning and a keen eye for detail enable me to deliver high-quality work that exceeds expectations. I am excited to contribute my skills and collaborate on exciting projects that push the boundaries of technology and design.
